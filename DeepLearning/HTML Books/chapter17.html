The Diffusion Process

📖 Two Processes

Forward: Gradually add noise to data until it becomes pure noise
Reverse: Learn to gradually remove noise, recovering data

Training: predict the noise that was added at each step.

Denoising Diffusion (DDPM)

x_t = √(α_t) x_0 + √(1-α_t) ε

A neural network learns to predict ε given x_t and timestep t.

Modern Diffusion Models

  • DDPM: Original diffusion formulation
  • DDIM: Faster deterministic sampling
  • Stable Diffusion: Latent space diffusion, text conditioning
  • DALL-E 2/3: Text-to-image generation

Key Takeaways

  • Diffusion: forward adds noise, reverse removes it
  • Train network to predict noise at each step
  • State-of-the-art image quality
  • Powers Stable Diffusion, DALL-E, Midjourney
  • Can be conditioned on text, images, other signals
