The VAE Framework

📖 VAE Components

Encoder: q(z|x) - Maps data to latent distribution (mean and variance)
Decoder: p(x|z) - Maps latent code to reconstructed data
Latent space: Continuous, structured representation

The ELBO Objective

ELBO = E[log p(x|z)] - KL(q(z|x) || p(z))

Reconstruction term + regularization toward prior (standard Gaussian).

Reparameterization Trick

To backpropagate through sampling: z = μ + σ ⊙ ε, where ε ~ N(0,I)

Key Takeaways

  • VAEs learn smooth, continuous latent spaces
  • ELBO balances reconstruction and regularization
  • Reparameterization enables gradient-based training
  • Enable interpolation and attribute manipulation
