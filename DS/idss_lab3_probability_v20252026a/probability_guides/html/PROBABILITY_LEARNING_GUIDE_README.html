🗺️ Recommended Learning Path

📍 Step 1: Foundation (Guides 1-2)

Start with PMFs and Conditional Probability to build your foundation. These are essential for everything that follows.

📍 Step 2: Core Concepts (Guides 3-4)

Master Bayes' Rule and Entropy - two of the most powerful tools in probability theory.

📍 Step 3: Practical Methods (Guide 5)

Learn Monte Carlo methods to solve real-world problems through simulation.

📍 Step 4: Advanced Topics (Guides 6-8)

Dive into multivariate distributions, parameter estimation, and optimization techniques.
