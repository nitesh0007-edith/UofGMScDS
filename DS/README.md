## Lab Coursework

### Lab 3 — Probabilistic Inference
**Folder:** `idss_lab3_probability_v20252026a/`

Notebook implementing Bayesian search for a lost submarine using probability mass functions, conditional probability, and Monte Carlo sampling. Includes probability study guides (PDF, HTML, Markdown) covering:
- PMF & discrete probability
- Conditional probability & Bayes' Rule
- Entropy & information theory
- Sampling & Monte Carlo methods
- Multivariate normal distributions
- Maximum likelihood estimation
- Stochastic optimisation
