How to Write Exam Answers (from Dr. McCreadie's guidance)

  • Context matters: Half the marks are for the concept, half for linking it to the scenario. Always connect your answer to the question's context.
  • "Discuss/Describe" = write short paragraphs. Rule: marks ÷ 2 ≈ number of sentences.
  • "List" = bullet points. Look for "X points expected" in guidance.
  • 2026 exam: Closed book with crib sheet (Spark functions only). 3 questions, 60 marks, 1.5 hours.
  • Structure: Q1 = theory (weeks 1-2), Q2 = distributed compute, Q3 = distributed data.
